# Discovery notes — Helping Hands NGO volunteer demo

Meeting: 2026-05-15 with Maya (Volunteer Coordinator) and Tom (Programs Director).

## What they want to show

- Volunteers should be able to find open shifts on a public landing page without logging in. Maya says this is the #1 friction point — right now they email shift lists and people lose them.
- A volunteer signs up for a shift in two clicks, gets a confirmation, and the system auto-creates a Person Account if they're new.
- Maya logs into Salesforce Monday morning and sees who signed up, with a flag on anyone who hasn't volunteered before. She wants to send those people a personal welcome.
- Tom wants a dashboard at the end showing: shifts filled this week, new volunteers this week, retention rate.

## Personas

- Maya, Volunteer Coordinator — spends ~3 hours every Monday reconciling shift signups across email + spreadsheet + Salesforce. She'd rather spend that time onboarding new volunteers personally.
- Sarah, prospective volunteer — heard about Helping Hands from a friend, doesn't have a Salesforce login, just wants to help out at a food drive Saturday.

## Constraints

- Must work on Nonprofit Cloud (not NPSP).
- Must use Experience Cloud public guest site for the signup page.
- Demo runs in 30 minutes — no longer.
- Don't show Setup. The audience is nonprofit operators, not admins.
