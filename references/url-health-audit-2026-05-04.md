# Upstream URL health audit (2026-05-04)

Generated by the weekly skill-refresh pipeline. This report captures
the health of every Salesforce-hosted `upstream_refs` URL across the
NGOSkills repo, as measured on 2026-05-04 by the Playwright-based
`extract_sf_doc.py` extractor.

## Context

Running the refresh pipeline against the 71 `sf-*` skills whose
`upstream_refs` point exclusively at `developer.salesforce.com`,
`help.salesforce.com`, and `architect.salesforce.com` surfaced a
large URL-rot problem:

- **178 unique URLs audited**
- **57 hard 404** (roughly 32%)
- **56 empty-shell** (roughly 31%) — return HTTP 200 but serve
  Salesforce's "page not found" landing
- **59 live content** (roughly 33%)
- **6 other errors**

Root cause: Salesforce has been aggressively reorganizing their doc
surfaces. Notable patterns retired:

- `architect.salesforce.com/design/<product>` — entire path retired
- Many `developer.salesforce.com/docs/einstein/genai/` pages 404
- Many `help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=<slug>.htm` article IDs
  consolidated under new slugs; old URLs serve the page-not-found shell
